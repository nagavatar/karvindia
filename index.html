ABOUT KARV & ASSOCIATES LLP

KARV & ASSOCIATES LLP is a renowned Chartered Accountants firm established in the year 2012. We are a team of Chartered Accountants and other professionals in India with relevant knowledge and professional experience of rendering comprehensive professional services.

Our Core Services Include:

  • Audit and Assurance Services - Statutory and internal audits, attestation services
  • Taxation and Advisory Services - Income tax planning, compliance, and representation
  • International Taxation & Transfer Pricing - Global taxation strategies
  • GST and Compliance Services - GST registration, returns, and advisory
  • Transaction Advisory Services - M&A support and business valuations
  • Company Law and Secretarial Matters - Corporate governance and compliance
  • Project Financing and Financial Advisory - Financing solutions and financial planning
  • Risk Management and Accounts Outsourcing - Comprehensive financial management

Our firm represents a combination of specialized skills and industry expertise, which enables us to offer sound financial advice and personalized proactive services to clients across various industries.
